## WHO IS STAVARAI

Stavarai is a young professional working inside a social media management company.
He and his partner are not founders — they are employees who are building the
infrastructure that will make them indispensable, then acquirable.

They are the two people who understand how this platform works.
The company sees results. Not the engine.

---

## THE COMPANY (internal name only — never expose publicly)

A social media management agency serving Shopify brands across:
- Food & Beverage
- Beauty & Skincare
- Apparel & Accessories
- Home & Lifestyle

Their clients need: consistent content, viral-ready video, engaged audiences,
and proof that social media is driving sales — not just vanity metrics.

The company's pain: they're scaling clients faster than their team can produce
quality content. They need a system, not more headcount.

---

## THE PLATFORM'S ROLE

This platform solves the company's problem before Stavarai asks for anything.
It demonstrates value so clearly that:

1. Clients start getting better results
2. The company starts depending on the output
3. Stavarai and partner become the only two people who understand why
4. The acquisition conversation starts with the company asking, not Stavarai pitching

**Built to Sell positioning:**
- The platform runs without Stavarai present
- The SOPs are in `docs/SOPs/`
- The data moat is the proprietary training on company client history
- The blog builds public authority for the "AI social media experts"
- The $500K ask includes: cash + stock + a retained role for 12 months

---

## THE BLOG STRATEGY (Sticky Point)

The blog serves multiple audiences simultaneously:

**For clients (Shopify brands):**
- How to build stores that convert
- Avoiding AI-generated slop in product descriptions
- Social media strategy specific to their niche
- Case studies (anonymized) showing results

**For the company:**
- Positioning Stavarai + partner as thought leaders
- Internal SOPs made readable for non-technical stakeholders
- Proof of expertise before the acquisition conversation

**For affiliate revenue (phase 2):**
- Shopify tools and apps
- AI generation tools
- Social media scheduling tools (Buffer affiliate link)
- Email marketing platforms

**Content calendar:** 2 posts/week. Written by the Hermes autoresearch agent.
Reviewed by Stavarai. Published when approved.

## THE ACQUISITION PITCH (when ready)

Framing: "You already depend on this. The only question is ownership."

Assets:
- Platform codebase (proprietary, not open source)
- 12+ months of client performance data
- Trained scoring models on company-specific content
- Blog with Domain Authority + affiliate revenue
- SOPs that any new employee can follow
- Stavarai + partner for 12-month transition

Ask: $500K — structured as:
- $200K cash upfront
- $150K in company stock (cliff: 12 months)
- $150K in phantom equity or performance bonus (tied to platform metrics)

Walk away point: below $350K total or less than 12-month retention offer.
